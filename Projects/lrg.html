This project was an introductory project to HTML, CSS, and Javascript without the use of any additional frameworks or libraries. The goal of this project was to create an e-commerce website for a small. bakery, Bun Bun Bakeshop where customers could browse and shop for their specialty cinnamon buns.

I wanted this website to contain the traditional home page, product browsing page, product detail page, shopping cart, and about us page. While doing so, I also wanted to create a cozy environment for the customers on the website to relate the experience to buying these products in person as much as possible. I used a warm and neutral color palette and tried to be as personal as a business can be.
